About the Sangfor EPIC Cloud Summit

Get ready for an extraordinary experience at the highly anticipated Sangfor EPIC Cloud Summit 2026! This annual event offers unique opportunities for technological Exchange, Partnership, Innovation, and Connection, which are core values that Sangfor proudly brings to our customers and partners.

This year’s theme, Digital Today, AI Tomorrow, highlights the next phase of enterprise transformation, where today’s digital foundations pave the way for tomorrow’s AI-driven innovations. Discover how advancements in cloud and cybersecurity are coming together to help organizations unlock new efficiencies, strengthen resilience, and accelerate their AI journey.

Join Sangfor executives and leading IT experts at the EPIC Cloud Summit. Explore the latest developments in cloud infrastructure and cybersecurity, while networking and exchanging ideas with industry peers. Expect insightful presentations, interactive demos, and forward-looking discussions as Sangfor continues to make your digital & AI transformation simpler and more secure.

More details will be shared as the event approaches. Stay tuned and mark your calendar for this exciting event!
